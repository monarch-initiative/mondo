
This is a request for a new term, RNU12-related minor spliceopathy disorder, by the ClinGen team.

### Term label and synonyms

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Preferred term label** | RNU12-related minor spliceopathy disorder |
| **Synonyms** | craniosynostosis-anal anomalies-porokeratosis syndrome, CDAGS syndrome, spinocerebellar ataxia, autosomal recessive 33 |


### Definition

Abnormal splicing of pre-mRNA caused by variants in the RNU12 gene. This term captures the disease spectrum. The term includes craniosynostosis-anal anomalies-porokeratosis, also known as CDAGS syndrome, and spinocerebellar ataxia, autosomal recessive 33.
